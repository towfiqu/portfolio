About

Always excited to learn & build new things.

Self-taught full stack web developer from Dhaka, Bangladesh. I have been working in web development for last two years and absolutely loving it since.

Over the last two years I have created various Projects in web with Html, CSS, JavaScript, React, NodeJS, MongoDB and MySQL. Occasionally, I also work with WordPress and UX design.

I built this site with HTML5, CSS3 & JavaScript. Hosted on Netlify.

Although, I work with Full Stack, I am particularly passionate about Front-End web development. Specially working with CSS, JavaScript and Responsive design. Improving speed and performance of a site are two of the things I am very fond of.

I write about web development, mostly on medium. All of my posts can be found on articles section of this site.

When I am not writing code or fixing a bug. I am probably watching a movie or reading a book. Feel free to give me a buzz if you are interested to work with me.
